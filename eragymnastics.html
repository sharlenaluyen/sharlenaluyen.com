Competitive Programs

ERA Team

ERA athletes compete locally and across the country. We build great work habits, team-building skills, a love for the sport, and life-long friendships. Team positions are by invitation only.

Ages 5 – 9

Pre-Team

Introduced to the structure of team gymnastics through event coaching, strength training, and flexibility. Athletes have the potential to compete when they achieve all necessary skills with correct form and a great work ethic.

Xcel Program

Xcel Team

An alternative to the Developmental Program that introduces athletes to the optional program. ERA offers levels Bronze through Diamond.

Bronze/Silver: 4–6 competitions/year · 4–6 hrs/week
Gold/Platinum/Diamond: 5–10 competitions · 8–12 hrs/week
Ages 7 – 18

Developmental Team

A serious commitment to the sport. Athletes train significantly more and progress faster while developing greater strength, flexibility, and technique. ERA currently offers levels 4–6.

Compulsories (Lvl 4/5): 12 hrs/week
Optionals (Lvl 6–10): 12–20 hrs/week
How does placement work? Every ERA gymnast is considered a candidate for a team spot. Athletes are observed discreetly throughout their time at ERA — we assess strength, flexibility, listening skills, and more. We will contact you if your gymnast is selected as the best candidate for an open position. Tryouts are held once a year and are invite only.
