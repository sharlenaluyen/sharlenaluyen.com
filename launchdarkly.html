The "Why"

Why LaunchDarkly

Not a generic cover letter — a genuine breakdown of what draws me to this company and this work.

🔬

I want to live where 0.05ms matters

Over 20 trillion flag evaluations per day. That's not just engineering — that's physics. Working in Go on hot paths where every microsecond has real consequences is exactly the environment I want to be building in.

🌐

Distributed systems is where I want to live

Kinesis, Flink, Clickhouse, global AWS footprint — the LaunchDarkly platform is a masterclass in distributed systems design at real scale. I'm genuinely obsessed with how these systems stay consistent and resilient when the whole world is talking to them.

🏠

Remote-first, not remote-tolerated

I have only ever worked remotely. It's not a perk I'm hunting for — it's the way I do my best work. LaunchDarkly is built remote-first by design, not a traditional company with a Zoom option, and that difference matters deeply to me.

🔥

You build it, you run it

On-call rotations and production ownership aren't a downside to me — they're the point. Engineers who carry the pager write fundamentally better software. A culture where operational toil is treated as a bug to fix speaks my language.
