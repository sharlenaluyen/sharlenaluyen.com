Why ActBlue

I don't just want a job; I want a mission.

As a software engineer and longtime civic volunteer, I want to build systems that matter — ones that help millions of ordinary people participate in their democracy.

  • I've implemented and maintained large scale distrbuted systems in infrastructure at HashiCorp, an IBM company. Primarily as a backend engineer during this time, I built a system for cross identity management between external identity providers and HashiCorp Cloud Platform.
  • I believe engineering is empathy for the user: building for stressed-out campaign staffers, first-time donors, and everyone in between is just as important as translating business needs into technical requirements.
  • As a previous software consultant, I helped implement Move HQ in Ruby on Rails and React to support replacing the government's legacy systems for helping military folks move. I've had the opportunity to work with diverse teams and stakeholders to deliver solutions that meet both technical and business needs.
  • I bring a culture of trust, humility, and shared accountability; the same values ActBlue builds their team around. Ideally, I'm looking for a new role where I am allowed to make mistakes, learn, and grow.
